The world is constantly changing and growing, and so are all the wonderful things to learn
in it. From changing perspectives on history and studies of people to the world of deep
AI and computer science, there’s more to know than ever before.

When you’re out of school, however, it can be hard to find places to learn new things, create
new skills, and expand your horizons. The Internet is helping to solve this bymaking courses
and training easier to find than ever before. Online classes are the perfect way for most to
continue their education and we’ve put together a list of sources to help you find the best fit
for you.

1. Academic Earth

Academic Earth is a collection of free online college courses from some of the world’s leading
universities and colleges. On its site you’ll be able to find specific courses in many subjects as
well as search by university. It collects playlists and video groups to help you learn from many
different services, too.Academic Earth was launched on the premise that everyone deserves
access to a world-class education. In 2009, we built the first collection of free online college
courses from the world’s top universities. The world of open education has exploded sincethen,
so today our curated lists of online courses are hand selected by our staff to show you the
very best offerings by subject area.
